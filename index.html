This is Color Harmony

Color harmony is the combination of colors that look pleasing together. Colors can shape mood & perception. My design uses balanced pallettes and a gradient to create clarity.

This section uses a Gradient for its background, specifically a linear gradient.

Gradients are smooth transitions between two or more colors. They create a sense of depth, movement, and modern style when used carefully.

This section uses a Solid Color for its background.
